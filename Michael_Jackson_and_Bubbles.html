JEFF KOONS
ARTWORKS ABOUT

Michael Jackson
and Bubbles

1988
42 x 70 1/2 x 32 1/2 in.
porcelain

'Michael Jackson and Bubbles', is a sculpture that portrays
the king of pop Michael Jackson and his pet monkey Bubbles.
The piece portrays the singer as a god like figure and depicts
the essence and culture of celebrity worship in the pop space.
