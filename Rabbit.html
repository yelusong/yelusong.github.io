JEFF KOONS
ARTWORKS ABOUT

Rabbit

1986
41 x 19 x 12 in.
stainless steel

'Rabbit', is an embodiment of the spirit at the time. The reflective surface of the ‘Rabbit’ means that as we view the sculpture we simultaneously view ourselves. It is, literally, a reflection of our own society.
The ‘Rabbit’ becomes a statement of the deception present in society, acting as a social commentary and critique of our mainstream culture and its focus on the image.
