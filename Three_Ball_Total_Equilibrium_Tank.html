JEFF KOONS
ARTWORKS ABOUT

Three Ball Total Equilibrium Tank

1985
60 1/2 x 48 3/4 x 13 1/4 in.
glass, steel, distilled water and three basketballs

A state of equilibrium is when something is in perfect balance.
The basket balls float in what seems an unnatural state most
people have a hard time of comprehending. However, the balls
are not in a state of perfect equilibrium as the balls sink to the
bottom of the tank over time. This is however something that
would be hidden to the viewer unless one would revisit it over
time. The artist himself claims that the work is a representation
of death as the ultimate state of being.
