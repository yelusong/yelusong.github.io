JEFF KOONS
ARTWORKS ABOUT

Balloon Dog

1994-2000
121 x 143 x 45 in.
mirror-polished stainless steel
with transparent color coating

'Balloon Dog', is a sculpture that memorialize the rituals,
icons, and images surrounding birthdays, holidays, and
other party occasions. Koons has frozen a moment in
time, exaggerating both scale and material. This piece
is an aloof sentinel cloaked in nostalgia.
