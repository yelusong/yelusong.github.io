JEFF KOONS
ARTWORKS ABOUT

Play-Doh

1994-2014
120 × 108 × 108 in.
painted aluminum

Inspired by the artist's son, 'Play-Doh' is a sculpture
that is driven by a sense of sheer fun: the infectious
enthusiasm of an artist willing to strive for two decades
to recreate the appearance of Play-Doh on a vast, even
surreal scale — and the implied invitation for us not to
judge, but to join in. This sculture was created in order
to 'capture a feeling of creation'
